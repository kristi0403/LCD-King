Who Are We ?

OUR BEGINNINGS.

LCD KING (SY) ltd. was born in 2014 when our founder began refurbishing mobile phone LCD screens in his garden shed.

In just four years our business has grown to a team of eight members of staff in two business premises.

We are now one of the leading mobile phone screen repair specialists in the area, catering to a wide and varied client base.

WHAT WE DO?

As well as repairing phone screens, our specialists can provide a full diagnosis of any further issues with your device and rectify them. These may include:

  • Charging port repair
  • Front/rear camera replacement
  • Sim tray & power button issues
  • Battery replacements

We are one of few businesses offering “top-glass only” repairs, as well as a full LCD screen replacement service, which means that we can fix your device at a much lower price than our competitors!

Why Us?

Here at LCD King (SY) we use only genuine screens and parts, so you can be sure your repaired device will be of the highest quality.

Our repairs usually take between half an hour and an hour to complete and we provide an in-house waiting room for you to relax in while you wait for your device to be fixed.

We provide a full three month service warranty in the unlikely event of any problems occurring. As well as walk in repairs.
